Visual Communication



800 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises help you learn several ways various programs can be used to faciliate professional visual business communications. Programs such as Microsoft PowerPoint, Sway, and YouTube have features to improve the quality of your visual communications, and make more efficient use of your time.

After completing this assignment you will be able to use these programs to:

  • generate powerful visual images
  • record videos and still photos from your laptop web camera
  • record a business video that tells a story
  • post to and embed videos using YouTube
Assignment Overview
This assignment will cover several basic visual communication functions, you are encouraged to explore on your own to find additional features that will make your life and present or future jobs easier.
Some things you can do include:
  • Develop a way to tell a compelling story through video
  • Use a video to quickly tell a powerful story
  • Place static text and images into PowerPoint, then create both a presentation and a video
