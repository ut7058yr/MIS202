Written Communication



700 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises help you learn several ways various programs can be used to faciliate written communications. Programs such as Microsoft OneNote, Word and Lync/Skype have features to improve the quality of your communications, and make more efficient use of your time.

After completing this assignment you will be able to use Word to:

  • create complex documents
  • develop a professional bio to introduce yourself to your classmates
  • create a resume-both document based and web based
  • use business class instant messaging to communicate with coworkers and clients
  • understand how to organize your work and research in an electronic notebook
Assignment Overview
This assignment will cover several basic written communication functions, you are encouraged to explore on your own to find additional features that will make your life and present or future jobs easier.
Some things you can do include:
  • Develop a way to market yourself through your bio
  • Using word processing applications for advanced functions, such as creating tables of contents
  • Using OneNote as an electronic notebook to organize your work and facilitate research

Make sure that Word and OneNote 2016 are properly setup on your computer before proceeding. If necessary, contact Tech Support in Somsen 207 for assistance.
